About Us

Why Ride?

  • Get fit!
  • Get outdoors and closer to nature!
  • Learn a new skill!
  • Meet new people!

What do I need?

  • We recommend shoes with a thick heel such as boots or wellies
  • Avoid flat shoes such as trainers
  • If riding outdoors be prepared for the Great British weather!
  • We provide riding hats in all sizes

Why Us?

  • Our instructors have many years of valuable experience
  • Beginers are guided every step of the way
  • Well trained and well cared for horses
  • For safety we never ride alone!
