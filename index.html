About Us

Why Ride?

Get fit. Get outdoors and closer to nature. Learn a new skill and meet new people.

What do I need?

We recommend shoes with a thick heel such as boots or wellies. Avoid flat shoes such as trainers If riding outdoors be prepared for the Great British weather! We provide riding hats in all sizes
